Chapter 1
The great kingdom of the forest ring once created 4 massive gates and tasked 4 immensely powerful beings with guarding them. The mountain giant champion Smashmir of the mountain giant clans was guardian of the east gate, the earth colossus, a great being made of the earth itself, was guardian of the western gate, a great elf lord who's name I have forgot once was guardian of the south gate until it fell to goblins and the nameless knight was guardian of the north gate. These gates once guarded the hill ring and forest ring kingdom. Now they have fallen to an evil necromancer. The giant has become an undead giant skeleton, the colossus attacks anyone on sight, the nameless knight is now a creature of darkness and its said that the goblins are under the influence of the necromancer.
