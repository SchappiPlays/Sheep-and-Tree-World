SKILL TREE PLAN
===============

XP Sources:
- Kill goblin: 5 XP
- Kill skeleton: 8 XP
- Kill elite creature: 15 XP
- Kill boss: full level up (100 XP)
- Mine copper ore: 2 XP
- Mine iron ore: 3 XP
- Mine gold ore: 5 XP
- Mine diamond ore: 10 XP
- Mine gem ore (ruby/sapphire/emerald/topaz): 8 XP
- Buy from shop: 1 XP per 10 coins spent
- Sell to shop: 1 XP per 10 coins earned

Leveling:
- XP needed per level: 100 XP (flat)
- Each level grants 1 skill point
- Max level: 20 (20 skill points total)

===========================
WARRIOR TREE (10 upgrades)
===========================

1. Tough Skin
   - +10% damage reduction (flat)
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: nothing

2. Sharp Edge
   - +15% sword damage
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Tough Skin

3. Quick Strike
   - 20% faster sword attack cooldown
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Sharp Edge

4. Shield Mastery
   - Shields block 50% more damage, stagger bosses in 2 hits instead of 3
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Tough Skin

5. Berserker
   - Below 30% HP: +40% damage with all weapons
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Sharp Edge

6. Heavy Blow
   - 10% chance per sword hit to stun enemy for 1 second
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Quick Strike

7. Iron Stomach
   - Health potions heal 2 hearts instead of 1
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Shield Mastery

8. Warrior's Endurance
   - +2 max hearts (permanent)
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Berserker

9. Dual Threat
   - Sword attacks also deal damage behind you (360 degree)
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Heavy Blow

10. Warlord
    - +30% damage with all weapons, +20% damage reduction
    - Cost: 1 point
    - Requires: Warrior's Endurance + Dual Threat (both)

===========================
WIZARD TREE (10 upgrades)
===========================

1. Mana Well
   - +2 max mana (1 extra star)
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: nothing

2. Quick Cast
   - 25% faster staff attack cooldown
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Mana Well

3. Mana Flow
   - Passive mana regen doubled (0.5 → 1.0 per second)
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Mana Well

4. Spell Power
   - +20% staff/spell damage
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Quick Cast

5. Efficient Casting
   - Spells cost 1 less mana (minimum 1)
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Mana Flow

6. Chain Reaction
   - Bolt projectiles explode on hit, dealing 30% damage to nearby enemies
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Spell Power

7. Arcane Shield
   - Magic shield drains 50% less mana
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Efficient Casting

8. Spell Leech
   - Staff kills restore 2 mana
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Chain Reaction

9. Mana Surge
   - +4 max mana (2 extra stars)
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Requires: Arcane Shield

10. Archmage
    - All spells +40% damage, unlock spell slot 4/5/6 immediately
    - Cost: 1 point
    - Requires: Spell Leech + Mana Surge (both)
