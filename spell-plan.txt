SPELL SLOT SYSTEM REDESIGN
==========================

- Player has 9 spell slots (Shift+1 through Shift+9)
- Slot 1 (Bolt) and Slot 2 (Shield) are always locked in
- Slots 3-9 can be customized from unlocked spells
- Spells are unlocked via the wizard skill tree branches
- Each branch has a theme with 4-5 spells

WIZARD TREE BRANCHES
====================

CORE (always available):
  Slot 1: Bolt (projectile) — always equipped
  Slot 2: Shield (barrier) — always equipped

ELEMENTAL BRANCH:
  - Inferno (fire ring, 6 DMG AOE) [start of branch]
  - Frost Nova (freeze enemies 4s)
  - Chain Lightning (3 targets, 10 DMG each)
  - Meteor (massive sky AOE, 20 DMG)
  - Elemental Storm (random fire/ice/lightning rains for 8s)

ARCANE BRANCH:
  - Arcane Barrage (5 homing bolts)
  - Teleport (blink 15 units forward)
  - Crystal Storm (8 piercing shards)
  - Arcane Shield+ (shield reflects damage back)
  - Time Warp (slow all enemies 50% for 8s)

NECROMANCY BRANCH:
  - Raise Dead (summon skeletons, all mana)
  - Soul Drain (beam that damages enemy + heals you)
  - Curse (target takes 2x damage for 10s)
  - Wraith Form (become invulnerable + pass through blocks for 5s)
  - Army of the Dead (summon 8 skeletons regardless of mana)

NATURE BRANCH:
  - Heal (restore 4 HP)
  - Regrowth (heal 6 HP + regen)
  - Earthen Wall (block wall for 10s)
  - Entangle (roots hold enemies in place 5s)
  - Nature's Wrath (thorns + healing aura for 10s)

STORM BRANCH:
  - Storm Call (lightning rain 5s)
  - Tornado (pulls enemies in, 4 DMG/s for 6s)
  - Thunder Clap (knockback + stun all nearby 3s)
  - Avatar (10s double damage, no mana drain)
  - Tempest (massive storm: lightning + wind + rain for 12s)

WIZARD TREE LAYOUT:
  - Mana Well → Quick Cast → branches split:
    - → Spell Power → ELEMENTAL BRANCH
    - → Mana Flow → ARCANE BRANCH
    - → Efficient Casting → NECROMANCY BRANCH
    - → Arcane Shield → NATURE BRANCH
    - → Chain Reaction → STORM BRANCH
  - Mana Surge requires any 2 branches started
  - Archmage requires Mana Surge + any branch completed

TOTAL: 25 unlockable spells + 2 core = 27 spells
Player can equip any 7 into slots 3-9
